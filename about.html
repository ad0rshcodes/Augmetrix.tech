“You can’t use up creativity. The more you use, the more you have.”

We, at Augmetrix.tech, aim at fostering and making the right use of one’s creativity and imagination. We are a student-run non-profit organisation that helps high schoolers to gain the trending and need-of-the-hour vocational skills free of cost. We believe that India is going to be the next Global Superpower and to turn this belief into reality we wish to equip our future leaders with the right knowledge and wish to make them competent.

  • Don’t worry if you’re a beginner, we’ll be more than happy to have you
  • Be a part of workshops on video editing, programming, graphic designing and lots more
  • Receive timely updates on opportunities that might interest you

About Us

A non-profit organisation that is 100% passion oriented and intends to provide vocational skills to as many high schoolers as possible. We believe in a non-discriminating attitude and hence welcome girls, boys, beginners, amateurs, the privileged and underprivileged ones. Workshops, hackathons, sessions at various NGOs are just a small portion of things we aim at doing.
