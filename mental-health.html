Mental Wellness is an internal experience that helps us think, feel, connect and build. It is an active process that transforms us into a resilient, strong and wise person. It has multi-dimensional aspects which will help us understand our own selves in a better way, they are-

  1. Mental: How we think; how we process, understand, and use information.
  2. Emotional: How we feel; how we manage and express our emotions.
  3. Social: How we connect; our relationships with others.
  4. Psychological: How we act or function, or how we “put the pieces together;” taking external inputs along with our internal capacity and then making decisions or doing things.

Why do we need to consider mental wellness?

Mental Health includes our emotional , psychological and social well being , it helps us determine how we handle stress,relate to others and make choices. Mental Wellness can also be defined as the positive side of mental health . This lets us -

  1. Realise our full potential
  2. Cope with the stresses of life
  3. Work Productively
  4. Make meaningful contributions to their communities.

Working towards mental wellness

  1. Learn to be at peace with yourself
    • Get to know who you are and what makes you happy
    • Acknowledge what you can and cannot change about yourself
    • Identify your abilities and weaknesses, accept them, build on them, and do the best with what you have
  2. Pay attention to your own needs and feelings
    • Express your thoughts through a journal or blog to understand more about yourself
    • Write your thoughts in a diary , to get a clear solution out of overthinking .
  3. Work towards your objectives
    • Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-limited)
  4. Maintain a healthy lifestyle
    • Take balanced meals
    • Exercise regularly
  5. Build strong relationships
    • Make time for family and friends, and share life’s joys and sorrows with them
  6. Create a financial budget
    • Avoid overspending and focus on ‘needs’ instead of ‘wants’
  7. Volunteer for a cause
    • Being involved in the community gives a sense of purpose and satisfaction

Mental wellness is something ,which we must accept exists. It’s equal to physical wellness .
